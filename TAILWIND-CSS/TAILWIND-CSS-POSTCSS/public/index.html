Salt & Pepper Diamond Rings

We hand select unique salt and pepper diamonds so that even to the bare eye no two will look the same. Each of our 14K Gold settings are carefully designed to protect your diamond and to portray the story of your love.

These unique engagement rings can be customized to your liking. Create your perfect black diamond engagement ring in one of our pre-designed settings; select your desired metal color, choose your perfect center stone and we will take care of the rest!*

*Custom engagement rings will take 6-8 weeks to be produced.
